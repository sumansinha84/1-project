About PrijsWijzer

What this app does
PrijsWijzer lets you compare grocery prices across Dutch supermarkets. Enter your location (city, postcode, or address) and an item name to see which nearby store offers the best price.

How to use
Enter your location in the search box (or allow the app to use your current location), choose a radius (e.g. 5 km), type a product (e.g. potato, milk, bread), and click "Search prices in my area." Results show up to four supermarkets sorted by price. Click a card for more details, categories, and directions.

What you can check
You can search for items from major NL chains (Albert Heijn, Jumbo, Lidl, Aldi, Dirk, Plus, SPAR, Hoogvliet, and more). The app shows live or demo prices, distances, store addresses, and links to open maps for directions.
